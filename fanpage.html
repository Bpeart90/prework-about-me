OutKast

Info

Outkast is a rap duo from East Point, Atlanta, Georgia. The duo consist Andre "Andre 3000" Benjamin and Antwan "Big Boi" Patton. The group is known for helping to make Southern Hip Hop popular.

Hits

The Duo is known for it's many hits including Ms. Jackson, Hey Ya!, and The Way You Move. The group is also known for their collaberations with UGK and Killer Mike among others.

Awards and Careers

The Duo has released six studio albums and has sold over 25 million albums. The Duo has won six Grammys. The Duo also made a movie together in 2006 titled "Idlewild". The Duo took a hiatus in 2007, with Big Boi releasing solo albums and the both members would go on to prosue acting carrers. In 2014, the Duo reunited to perform a 40 festival tour together.
